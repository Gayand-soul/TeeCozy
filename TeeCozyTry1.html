About Us



We are a very genuine, cozy little tea shop.

Welcome to our cozy little tea shop, where the warmth of a freshly brewed cup of tea meets the charm of a peaceful atmosphere. We take pride in offering a wide selection of teas from all over the world, from delicate green teas to rich black teas, floral infusions, and rare herbal blends. Every tea is carefully prepared to ensure the perfect brew for each customer.

Our freshly baked treats of the day are made in-house and change with the weather and the chef’s mood. The selection is always a delightful surprise!

For every cup of tea ordered, we offer a choice of accompaniments – whether you prefer a slice of lemon, a touch of honey, or something a bit more indulgent, we’ll serve it just how you like it.

We value the moments of quiet relaxation that our customers seek. Our tea room is designed to provide a tranquil escape from the noise of everyday life. We kindly ask all visitors to respect the peaceful ambiance, so everyone can enjoy their time with us in comfort and serenity.

Take a moment to unwind and enjoy your tea with us!
